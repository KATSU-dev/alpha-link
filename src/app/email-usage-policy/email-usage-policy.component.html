Email Usage

General Email Usage

 

After creating an account, your email will be used for the following, as per the Terms and Conditions agreed by the user.

  • To contact the user regarding changes or updates to their account.
  • To contact the user in the event of a password reset.
  • To contact the user regarding suspicious behaviour on their account.
  • To contact the user in the event of a breach of the agreed Term and Conditions.

 

Opt-In Email Announcement Service

 

Should the user opt-in to this service, they may receive email communications such as the following.

  • General announcements made by the Alpha development team.
  • Information regarding changes between updated versions of the Alpha Link service.
  • Community engagement and outreach messages, such as competitions, giveaways, polls, etc.
